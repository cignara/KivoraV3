Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Kivora Learning ("Kivora", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our website at kivora.com and our learning platform.

1. Who We Are and How to Contact Us

Kivora Learning is an educational platform for children aged 2–11. For privacy questions or requests, contact us at: privacy@kivora.com

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Kivora is designed for children. We take the following measures to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and equivalent international laws:

  • No child accounts. Children do not create their own accounts. All accounts are created by parents or teachers (adults aged 18+).
  • Minimum data for children. We collect only a display name, age group, and chosen avatar for child profiles — no surname, no date of birth, no email address, no location.
  • Parental consent. A parent must create the account and explicitly add a child profile. This constitutes verifiable parental consent.
  • No advertising. Kivora displays zero advertisements. We do not sell, rent, or share child data with advertisers or marketing partners.
  • No social features. Children cannot communicate with other users, share content publicly, or interact with anyone outside their family/class.
  • Deletion rights. Parents can delete their child's profile and all associated data at any time from their dashboard, or by contacting us at privacy@kivora.com.

3. Data We Collect

Account Holders (Parents/Teachers)

  • Email address and display name (from your Google, Apple, or Microsoft account, or entered directly)
  • Profile photo (optional, from your SSO provider)
  • Account settings and preferences

Child Profiles

  • Display name (first name only recommended)
  • Age group (e.g. "Grade 2") — not exact birthdate
  • Chosen avatar emoji
  • Activity progress, XP earned, badges, and screen time sessions

Usage Data

  • Activities completed, time spent, scores, and session timestamps
  • Device type and browser (no IP address stored beyond 24 hours)
  • Firebase Analytics events (anonymised and aggregated)

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the Kivora learning platform
  • To generate progress reports and dashboard data for parents and teachers
  • To send weekly progress emails (if enabled, and only to the account holder)
  • To calculate rewards (XP, coins, badges, certificates)
  • To enforce screen time limits and bedtime locks set by parents
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore), hosted in secure Google Cloud data centres with enterprise-grade encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Access is controlled by Firebase Security Rules that prevent any user from accessing another user's data. We conduct regular security reviews.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained until the account is deleted
  • Child profile data is deleted when the child profile is removed by the parent
  • Session logs are automatically deleted after 12 months
  • Upon account deletion, all personal data is removed within 30 days

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, or Sri Lanka, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information from your dashboard
  • Deletion: Request erasure of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Request that we limit processing of your data
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact privacy@kivora.com. We respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

Kivora uses only essential cookies required for authentication (Firebase Auth session cookies) and user preferences (dark mode). We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies that identify individuals.

9. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and database hosting. See Firebase Privacy.
  • Google, Apple, Microsoft: Optional SSO providers. Only email and display name are shared with us. See their respective privacy policies.

We do not use advertising networks, social media pixels, or any third-party tracking technologies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We will notify account holders by email at least 14 days before making material changes to this policy. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
