Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Free plan really free forever?

Yes! No credit card required, no hidden fees, no time limit. The Free plan gives your child access to 50 activities, 10 stories and 20 coloring pages permanently.

Can I switch plans at any time?

Absolutely. Upgrade, downgrade or cancel anytime from your parent dashboard. If you cancel a paid plan, you keep access until the end of your billing period.

What age is Kivora suitable for?

Kivora is designed for children aged 2–11, from toddlers just starting to explore letters and numbers up to Grade 5 students tackling more complex topics.

Is Kivora COPPA and GDPR compliant?

Yes. Children never have their own accounts — all data is managed under the parent or teacher account. We collect minimum PII, no ads are shown, and parents can request data deletion at any time. See our Compliance page.

Do teachers get a discount?

Teachers at registered schools can access Kivora at institution pricing. Contact us via the Teachers page for school quotes and free trial arrangements.

What devices does Kivora work on?

Kivora works on any modern browser — desktop, tablet, and mobile. No app download required. An offline mode is coming soon for Explorer and Family plan members.
