Inclusive Learning Hub

Learning That Works for Every Child

Kivora Learning is built with universal design principles — every child, regardless of ability, learning difference or sensory need, can access our content comfortably and confidently.

♾️ Neurodiverse Friendly 🔊 Audio-First ♿ WCAG 2.1 🎯 Adjustable Pace
Platform Features

Built-In Accessibility Features

🔊

Audio-First Design

Every activity has full audio narration. Children can use Kivora Learning entirely by listening — no reading required for activities up to Grade 2.

🎯

Adjustable Pace

Parents can slow down activity pace and increase response time limits for children who need more processing time.

🎨

Visual Clarity

High-contrast colour options, dyslexia-friendly font mode, and simplified layouts available for children with visual processing differences.

🧩

Sensory-Friendly

No sudden loud sounds, no flashing animations, no timed pressure by default. All time-limits can be disabled by the parent.

📊

Granular Reporting

Track micro-progress for children who advance slowly. Every interaction logged so you can celebrate small wins meaningfully.

❤️

SEL & Emotional Support

Life Skills Village covers emotional regulation, mindfulness and social scenarios — essential for children with ADHD, autism and anxiety.

🏆

Flexible Rewards

The XP and badge system can be customised. Parents can set lower thresholds to celebrate smaller achievements and build confidence.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parent-Teacher Collaboration

Shared child progress data means parents and support teachers see the same picture and can coordinate learning goals.

By Profile

Supports a Range of Learning Profiles

Every child is unique. These are common ways Kivora Learning adapts — but many features combine to support the full spectrum of learners.

🎵

Autism Spectrum

Predictable structure, no social pressure, sensory-friendly defaults, and deep subject exploration for areas of special interest.

🧠

ADHD

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Kivora Learning ("Kivora Learning", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our website at kivora.com and our learning platform.

1. Who We Are and How to Contact Us

Kivora Learning is an educational platform for children aged 2–11. For privacy questions or requests, contact us at: privacy@kivora.com

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Kivora Learning is designed for children. We take the following measures to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and equivalent international laws:

  • No child accounts. Children do not create their own accounts. All accounts are created by parents or teachers (adults aged 18+).
  • Minimum data for children. We collect only a display name, age group, and chosen avatar for child profiles — no surname, no date of birth, no email address, no location.
  • Parental consent. A parent must create the account and explicitly add a child profile. This constitutes verifiable parental consent.
  • No advertising. Kivora Learning displays zero advertisements. We do not sell, rent, or share child data with advertisers or marketing partners.
  • No social features. Children cannot communicate with other users, share content publicly, or interact with anyone outside their family/class.
  • Deletion rights. Parents can delete their child's profile and all associated data at any time from their dashboard, or by contacting us at privacy@kivora.com.

3. Data We Collect

Account Holders (Parents/Teachers)

  • Email address and display name (from your Google, Apple, or Microsoft account, or entered directly)
  • Profile photo (optional, from your SSO provider)
  • Account settings and preferences

Child Profiles

  • Display name (first name only recommended)
  • Age group (e.g. "Grade 2") — not exact birthdate
  • Chosen avatar emoji
  • Activity progress, XP earned, badges, and screen time sessions

Usage Data

  • Activities completed, time spent, scores, and session timestamps
  • Device type and browser (no IP address stored beyond 24 hours)
  • Firebase Analytics events (anonymised and aggregated)

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the Kivora Learning learning platform
  • To generate progress reports and dashboard data for parents and teachers
  • To send weekly progress emails (if enabled, and only to the account holder)
  • To calculate rewards (XP, coins, badges, certificates)
  • To enforce screen time limits and bedtime locks set by parents
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore), hosted in secure Google Cloud data centres with enterprise-grade encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Access is controlled by Firebase Security Rules that prevent any user from accessing another user's data. We conduct regular security reviews.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained until the account is deleted
  • Child profile data is deleted when the child profile is removed by the parent
  • Session logs are automatically deleted after 12 months
  • Upon account deletion, all personal data is removed within 30 days

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, or Sri Lanka, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information from your dashboard
  • Deletion: Request erasure of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Request that we limit processing of your data
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact privacy@kivora.com. We respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

Kivora Learning uses only essential cookies required for authentication (Firebase Auth session cookies) and user preferences (dark mode). We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies that identify individuals.

9. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and database hosting. See Firebase Privacy.
  • Google, Apple, Microsoft: Optional SSO providers. Only email and display name are shared with us. See their respective privacy policies.

We do not use advertising networks, social media pixels, or any third-party tracking technologies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We will notify account holders by email at least 14 days before making material changes to this policy. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

Privacy Officer: privacy@kivora.com
Mailing address: Kivora Learning, Colombo, Sri Lanka.

Compliance

Our Compliance Commitments

How Kivora Learning meets COPPA, GDPR-K, Ofsted and international child safety standards.

Kivora Learning is designed from the ground up to be safe, compliant, and trustworthy for children and families worldwide. Below is a summary of the standards we meet and how we implement them.

COPPA — Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (USA)

  • Children do not create their own accounts. All accounts require a parent or teacher (18+).
  • Child profiles contain only a display name, age group, and avatar — no PII.
  • No behavioural advertising or third-party data sharing.
  • Parents can review, correct, or delete all child data at any time.
  • We do not condition participation on disclosure of more personal information than necessary.

GDPR-K — EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (Children)

  • Lawful basis for processing child data: parental consent (Art. 8, GDPR).
  • Data minimisation: we collect only what is necessary for the educational service.
  • Storage limitation: session logs deleted after 12 months; all data deleted on account closure within 30 days.
  • Rights of data subjects: access, rectification, erasure, portability, restriction, and objection — all supported via email request to privacy@kivora.com.
  • Data transfers: stored on Google Firebase (EU data centres available; see Firebase DPA).

UK Ofsted / DfE Safeguarding Standards

  • No child-to-child communication features.
  • No public content sharing by children.
  • Content reviewed for age-appropriateness by our editorial team before publication.
  • Screen time controls allow schools and parents to set daily limits in minutes.
  • All content is zero-violence, zero-adult themes, and free from commercial messaging.

FERPA — Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (USA)

  • School accounts require teacher setup; student data is only accessible to the teacher of record and the student's parent.
  • Educational records are not disclosed to third parties without parental consent.
  • Teachers can export student progress data for records purposes.

WCAG 2.1 — Web Content Accessibility Guidelines

  • Target: WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance across all content pages.
  • Audio narration for all activity instructions (screen reader supplement).
  • High contrast colour ratios (minimum 4.5:1 for text).
  • Keyboard navigation supported on all interactive elements.
  • Alt text on all images; ARIA labels on custom UI components.

Zero Advertising Policy

Kivora Learning displays no advertising of any kind. We do not use advertising networks, social pixels, or retargeting cookies. Children's data is never used for commercial purposes beyond providing the Kivora Learning service.

Data Security

  • All data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • Firebase Security Rules enforce per-user data isolation
  • Regular penetration testing and security audits
  • Incident response plan with 72-hour breach notification (per GDPR Art. 33)
