About Kivora

Built for Every Child on Earth

We believe every child deserves joyful, high-quality learning — no matter where they live or what they can afford. That belief built Kivora.

Our Mission

Learning Without Limits

Kivora creates magical educational worlds where every activity is an adventure, every answer builds confidence, and every session leaves a child eager to return. Our platform follows Common Core and UK National Curriculum frameworks while celebrating global cultural diversity.

Content is designed for children aged 2–11 across 9 worlds covering language, maths, science, creativity, coding, music, life skills, and critical thinking.
